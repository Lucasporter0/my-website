Everwell Supplements — SEO Content Hubs

Outdoor wellness DTC · 3-month sprint · Goal: non-brand growth ahead of peak season

Approach

  • Keyword map by intent (TOFU/MOFU/BOFU) and seasonality
  • 8 long-form category hubs + 24 supporting briefs
  • On-page fixes (titles, schema, FAQs) and internal link pass
  • “Size/guide” utility modules added to high-traffic posts

Stack

GA4, Google Search Console, Ahrefs, Sheets, Looker Studio

Outcomes (90 days)

  • +92% non-brand clicks (GSC)
  • +28% click-through rate to PLPs
  • +18% revenue from organic

Attribution: blended last-click + assisted from GA4.
